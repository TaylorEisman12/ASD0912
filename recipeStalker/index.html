  • Rock N Roll Crackers

    Ingredients:

  • 1 package Ranch salad dressing seasoning mix
  • 1 1/2 tbsp. Crushed red pepper
  • 1 tsp. Garlic powder
  • 1 package Saltine crackers
  • 1 1/2 cups Canola oil

    • Instructions:

    In a small bowl mix together salad seasoning mix, red pepper flakes, and garlic powder. Set aside. Place crackers in a large gallon sized sealable container, drizzle with canola oil. Add dry ingredients and seal container. Gently roll container to coat crackers with seasonings.

    Gently roll crackers every 30 minutes for 4 hours for best results.

  • 1 lb. Pan sausage

  • 8 oz. Grated cheddar cheese

    • Instructions:

  • Popeye's Biscuits

    Ingredients:

  • 4 cups Bisquick
  • 3/4 cup 7-Up
  • 8 oz Sour cream
  • 1 Stick butter

    • Instructions:

    Combine bisquick, 7-up, and sour cream. Stir until lumps are gone. Cut stick of butter in half. Melt 1/2 stick of butter in 9x13 or 9x9 pan. Drop biscuits by tablespoon into pan.

    Bake at 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes or until brown. Melt remainder of butter and baste the tops of each biscuit. Let stand for about 15 minutes and then serve.

  • Bull's Eyes

    Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

    • Instructions:

    Use the opening of a small glass or jar to cut the center out of a slice of bread. Keep the cut out center. Butter a skillet or pan.

    Using medium heat on the stove place the circle cut out and the bread slice onto the buttered pan. Crack the egg in the center of the bread, season with salt and pepper. Flip till egg is cooked and cut out toasted. Cut out is great for soaking up the runny yolk if not cooked through out.
